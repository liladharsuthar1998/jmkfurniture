About JMK Furniture

JMK Furniture is a family-run, Ahmedabad-based premium carpentry and interior firm. With decades of tradition in craftsmanship, we deliver turnkey bungalow interiors — from concept and design to build and finishing. Our focus is on durable joinery, elegant design and timely delivery.

  • Custom wardrobes, dressing units & beds
  • Modular kitchens and storage solutions
  • Full bungalow interior fit-outs
  • Material & finish guidance — teak, veneer, laminate, polish
